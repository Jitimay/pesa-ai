# Pesa AI — Final Pitch Script
**Event:** HashKey Chain On-Chain Horizon Hackathon 2026
**Track:** PayFi (Top 7 Finalist)
**Speaker:** Josue

---

### 🎬 SCENE 1 — THE HOOK (0:00 – 0:30)
**Action:** *Hold up the basic feature phone. Pause for 2 seconds.*

“This is not a smartphone. 
This is the most common financial tool in Africa. 
Growing up in Burundi, I watched people walk for hours just to send money—losing 6 to 10% in fees every single time. 
The internet wasn't there. But SMS was.”

---

### ⚡ SCENE 2 — THE VISION (0:30 – 0:55)
“We built **Pesa AI**—a system that turns any phone into a blockchain payment terminal. 
Users can send money, pay merchants, or pay bills using simple natural language. 
No internet, no smartphone, and no bank account required. 
We aren't just sending messages; we are turning 160 characters into a global bank account. 
Let me show you live.”

---

### 📱 SCENE 3 — THE LIVE DEMO (0:55 – 2:05)
**Action:** *Pick up the phone. Type/Show as you speak.*

“I’m sending a real payment in Kirundi: **‘Rungika 5 HSP kuri mama’**—Send 5 HSP to my mom.
**[Send SMS] [Turn focus to the Web Terminal/Explorer Screen]**

“Our system receives the SMS via our hardware bridge. In the background, **Groq AI** parses the intent. 
**Crucially, Pesa AI resolves the alias 'Mama' to her registered wallet address automatically.** 
It simultaneously runs our **AI Fraud Shield**, checking risk parameters to ensure compliance before the transaction ever touches the chain. 
Once cleared, it triggers an instant settlement via the **HashKey Settlement Protocol (HSP)**.”

**Action:** *Show the "Confirmed" status on the UI or HashKey Explorer.*

“And there it is. Settled on HashKey Chain in seconds for a fraction of a cent. 
The receiver gets an SMS notification. The loop is closed.”

---

### 🔥 SCENE 4 — THE "PAYFI" IMPACT (2:05 – 2:25)
“Today, cross-border remittances in Africa cost up to 10 percent. 
With Pesa AI, that cost drops to **0.1 percent**. 
For a family sending $100 home, that’s $9 saved every month. 
But more importantly, we are capturing the **'Missing Middle'**—the billions in transaction volume that legacy banks ignore because their overhead is too high. **Pesa AI makes the unbanked profitable.**”

---

### 🧠 SCENE 5 — HOW IT WORKS (2:25 – 2:45)
“The architecture is simple but powerful: **SMS → AI → HSP.** 
Groq handles the natural language and **Alias Resolution**—mapping phone numbers to wallets so users never have to see a 0x address. 
HashKey Chain provides the institutional-grade settlement. 
**Without blockchain, this is just a message. With blockchain, it becomes money.**”

---

### 💰 SCENE 6 — THE BUSINESS CASE (2:45 – 3:05)
“Users load and off-ramp funds through existing local agent networks. 
We earn a small, scalable transaction fee while remaining 90% cheaper than any bank. 
This is a high-volume bridge that brings millions of new users and real transaction volume into the HashKey ecosystem.”

### 🚀 SCENE 7 — THE CLOSE (3:05 – 3:25)
**Action:** *Hold up the phone again.*

“This phone cannot run a DeFi app. It cannot open a browser. 
But today, it just sent money across the world on the blockchain. 
We are not building another wallet. 
**[Pause 2 seconds, look directly at the judges]**
We are building access.”
